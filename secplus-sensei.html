ℹ️ About SecPlus Sensei

v1.1.7/div>

What this is

A CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701) study tool — flashcards with spaced repetition, practice quizzes, full exam simulations, and acronym drills, plus a dashboard that tracks your exam readiness. The whole thing works offline; no account, no signup, ever. Readiness % and simulated exam scores are in-app estimates — not official CompTIA metrics.

✨ How the optional AI works

"Optimize", the Card tutor, and quiz explanations are an optional bonus powered by Claude, using your own free Anthropic API key. Your key and your study progress are saved only on this device — the only network calls are your direct requests to Anthropic when you use an AI feature. Nothing is uploaded anywhere else.

FAQ & known issues

  1. Do I need an account? No — no signup or login, ever.
  2. Is my progress backed up? Yes — use Manage → Export backup anytime to save a copy. If you've saved an AI key, the backup contains it in plain text, so keep the file private.
  3. AI features aren't responding? Check your API key on the Dashboard and your internet connection — everything else in the app works fine without it.
  4. App feels out of date? It checks for updates automatically when you open it. If a new version is ready, you'll see a small banner — restart when you want, or it installs quietly when you close the app.
